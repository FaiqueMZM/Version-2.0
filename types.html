Types Of Tea

All four types of teas came from the same plant called 'Camellia sinensis', but the preparing method is different from one to another.

Green Tea

Green Tea is made from 'Camellia sinensis' leaves and buds.It is originated in China. Now its production and manufacture has spread all over the World, but it is high in Ease Asia. Several varieties of green tea there, which differ based on 'Camellia sinensis' leaves usage.

Green Tea has been used as traditional Indian and Chinese medicine. It has plenty of health benefits within it. It linked with Heart benefits, weight loss, reduced morality and more...

Black Tea

Black Tea is more oxidized than other three tea types and it is also generally stronger in flavor than those three tea. Green Tea usually loses its flavor within a year but, Black Tea retains its flavor for several years.

black Tea has Antioxidant Properties that helps to remove free radicals and decrease cell dmage in our body. It can boost the Health of Heart. It helps to reduce Blood Pressure and the Risk of Stroke.

White Tea

White Tea is one of the most delicate tea variety. For whit tea the buds and unflured leaves are picked when they just started growing so the leaves are not oxidize as long as the leaves picked for Black and Green Tea.

As like Green and Black Tea it also has benefits like Rich is Antioxidants, Reduces Risk of Heart diseases, helps in Weight loss and more...

Oolang Tea

Oolang Tea is a Traditional Semi-Oxidized Chinese Tea. It produced through a process including withering the plant under strong sun and oxidation before curling and twisting. Diiferent styles of Oolang Tea can vary widely in flavor.

Oolang Tea has benifits for brain, heart, born and dental health. It also may boost Metabolism and Decrease the Risk of developing Type-2 Diabetes.
