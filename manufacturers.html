DILMAH

Dilmah is a Sri Lankan Brand of Tea, sold worlwide. The company was founded in 1988 by "Merrill J Fernando".In 1974 Fernando bought his first tea estate in Sri Lanka and He founded 'Ceylon Tea Services Limited' in 1981. Dilmah is in over 100 countries worlwide. In 2009 Dilmah was considered as the 6th Largest Tea Brand in the World.

Founder Fernando had a unique vision that he wanted to offer consumers the finest tea on earth and to do so with Ethicks and Integrity. Dilmah is a unique brand that founded on a passionate commitmentto quality and authenticity in tea.
